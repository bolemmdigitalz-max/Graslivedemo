Our Story

Where Lagos Meets the World at the Table

GRÄS is an upscale all-day dining concept perched atop Cube 65 in Victoria Island — one of Lagos's most iconic addresses. With a display Ferrari at the entrance and a rooftop view that stretches across the city, the experience begins long before the first course.

We serve a curated fusion of world cuisines with a distinctly Nigerian soul. From Black Angus ribeye cooked to a perfect medium rare to the ethereal Butterfly Salmon, every dish is an expression of craft, quality, and intentionality.
